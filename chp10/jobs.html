To share and leverage information across StepStone Group's 14 offices and global client base, multiple web applications are used to report historical performance and make better investment decisions. We are looking for a front-end developer to join our quickly growing team and assist with the expansion and maintenance of these web applications.

The primary focus will be on front-end development, although the candidate should also be proficient in PHP and MySQL to facilitate reading and modifying the underlying data.
The developer position will be office-based (i.e., not remote) and located in La Jolla, California.
